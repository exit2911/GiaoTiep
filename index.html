GiaoTiep App

Đây là phần mềm cơ bản giúp những người tự kỷ không có khả năng nói giao tiêp. Phần mềm này vẫn đang tiếp tục phác triển.

This is a basic version of a rapid prompting method application designed to help nonverbal autistic children and adults communicate. The app is still being developed and more features will be added in the near future.

Contact Information: creating.noww@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

GiaoTiep is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of its users. We recognize that there are many situations where privacy can be at stake. We do our best to minimize the risks of compromising someone's privacy in our applications, support system, and the information we provide through our website and social media channels. If you have any concerns regarding the way we deal with privacy, or any related topic, please feel free to address them to creating.noww@gmail.com.

IN GENERAL

GiaoTiep does not use its iOS application to collect any personal information, such as but not limited to personal details, conversation transcripts, or documents. Nor does GiaoTiep provide third parties access to such information. The application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the application and application version, the type of device, the operating system version, settings and location. This information is in no way connected to your personal information.

GiaoTiep SUPPORT

In contact with our support team users typically share more personal information. This information is used to help the user with his specific problem or question. Furthermore the information can be used to improve AssistiveWare's products. In this case the personal information will only be used internally for development purposes.

GiaoTiep WEBSITE

We are not currently collecting any user information when users visit our website. Note that this privacy statement may change at any time. We will alert users if there are any changes. This privacy statement was last updated on: 21 September 2020.
